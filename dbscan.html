DBSCAN

This demo illustrates the DBSCAN function of ITSegmenter.js. It identifies and group clusters of points based on the parameters provided.

There are 4 parameters that you could change to affect the output:
        1. Thresh is the threshhold for FAST (Features from Accelerated Segment Test) corner detection
        2. Episilon is the maximum distance that 2 points can form a cluster
        3. Min Points is the minimum amount of points required to form a cluster
        4. Diameter is a parameter for Unsharp Masking (USM), it determines the area around each pixel that will be analyzed
        5. Amount is a parameter for Unsharp Masking (USM), it is a scalar for the unsharp mask, it determine the intensity of the edges
